Welcome to GenDE

Genomic Data Submission & Tracking

Follow these simple steps to submit your genomic data, track progress, and receive your sequencing results.

Overview

Learn the guidelines for submitting genomic data securely and efficiently. Ensure your files meet the required format and metadata standards.

Submit Data

Upload your genomic data through our submission portal. Provide necessary details such as sample ID, sequencing type, and consent information.

Track Progress

Monitor the status of your submission in real-time. Get updates on quality checks, sequencing progress, and analysis stages.

Receive Results

Once sequencing is complete, securely download your genome data and analysis report. Notifications will be sent to your registered email.

Need Help?

Our support team is available to assist with submission issues, tracking queries, or data interpretation guidance.
